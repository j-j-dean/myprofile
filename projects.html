Gamma Ray Observatory

I came on board this project a year before launch of the satellite. Most of the software to support spacecraft operations were complete. I learned how to work with a team to correct discrepencies. I learned about the software life cycle through experience and through on site training classes. I was responsible for implementing new requirements and updating the corresponding documentation for the user (user's guide and design documents). I was instrumental in developing software to perform limit testing allowing the flight operations team to monitor the health and safety of the spacecraft. The software was developed in FORTRAN using a Perkin-Elmer computer. On site at NASA we had removable disc packs that housed the software for various spacecraft that were scheduled around the clock to make use of limited resources.
