History:

I was born in the small town of Lockport, N.Y. The town is named for the locks that were built to allow ships to change elevation as they traveled the Erie canal transporting goods from the Great Lakes to NY city.


During my primary school years my father's job moved us to various locations throughout the country: Houston, TX; Philadelphia, PA; and Portsmouth, VA. I graduated from Churchland High School in Portsmouth, VA and attended the College of William and Mary where I met the love of my life.


After college we obtained employment in central Maryland. It was a great place to live and work. I thoroughly enjoyed working on NASA projects for various companies in the space industry. It was challenging and fulfilling.


With our growing family we moved from Maryland closer to family in Richmond, VA where we still live today. As a part of our move I took on the role as the Stay-At-Home-Dad. And it has been my joy to serve our family in this way.
